Overview:
This project simulates how farm crop yields of grains, fruits, and vegetables change 
over every four years, for a hundred years. The simulation will allow you to affect 
data from the simulation, in order to directly compare statistics.

Implementation:
An std::array of lists is used to store the lists of different yields of grains, 
fruits, and vegetables. An std::map is used to store the name of the farm as the 
key, and the three array of lists as its value. 

Simulation:
The simulation will involve changes in the weather for the year, such as increased 
rainfall or heat, which will either positively or negatively impact crop yield. 
As well, pests and disease could affect the yield of the crop.
